CASA AR – Augmented Reality Home Visualization

CASA AR is an augmented reality application that transforms how users plan and design their homes. It offers a two-phase experience:

3D Model Generation – Converts traditional 2D floor plans into interactive 3D models for spatial layout and visualization.
AR Walkthrough – Allows users to explore their future home using AR, with real-time furniture placement and cost estimation.

Goal:
To help homeowners and designers make smarter decisions by visualizing interiors and layouts before construction begins.
